Overview

I'm an aspiring Data Analyst based in Hyderabad, India. A majority of my time is spent in improving myself, mentally, physically and professionally. My fascination with creating dashboards and innate curiousity led me to the field of Data Analytics where I fell in love to the point that it keeps me up at night.

I've always been fascinated by problem solving, and working in data analytics has been a long-term goal of mine. My career in the customer-facing field and full stack development background has taught me to think strategically about problems, ask the right questions and identify solutions. I am excited to bring my technical and analytical skills to the field of data analysis.

In my free time, I enjoy exploring new coffee flavours, spending time with my family and friends and indulge in painting.
